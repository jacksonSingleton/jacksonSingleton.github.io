Development

Prototype Development

Most of my development experience comes from prototyping new possible ideas and testing new technologies. This ranges anywhere from web stack technologies with newer front-end frameworks, application development with Node.js, and business logistic management with CRM implementation and adaptation.

Tools Development

Another area of familiarity to me is building internal tools to help simplify repetitive tasks. If utilized correctly, software tools can create a more efficient workplace and help work out any kinks or bottlenecks in workflow. For example, I built a program that could generate 3D models based on set specifications and the company went from producing 3 models per day to over 20.

Web Development

Web development is my most comfortable skill. I have been working in HTML and CSS for nearly 5 years. I have experience with front and back-end frameworks such as Angular.js, React.js, and Ruby on Rails. I also have experience working in Content Management Systems such as WordPress and ModX.
